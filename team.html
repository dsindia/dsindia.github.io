Our Team

Founded at UC Berkeley, our organization has grown to include students from institutions around the world (Boston, London, Delhi, Hong Kong, and others) studying a diverse set of disciplines, ranging from computer science, math and engineering, to psychology, economics, and international relations. Just as the use of data unites the fields we pursue, we are united by a desire to spread our awareness of data to generate meaningful social change.
