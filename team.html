Our Team

Founded at UC Berkeley, Data Science for India is a team of students from institutions around the world studying a diverse set of disciplines, ranging from computer science, math, and engineering to psychology, anthropology, economics, and international relations. Ubiquitous data and the pressing need for data science in India is a common thread in the fields we pursue, and we are united by a desire to spread our awareness of data science to generate meaningful social change.
